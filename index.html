Search Engine Optimization

"SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, is the art and science of increasing a website's visibility on search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. By strategically optimizing content, keywords, and website structure, SEO aims to improve organic (non-paid) search engine rankings, driving more targeted traffic to a website. It involves techniques such as keyword research, on-page optimization, link building, and technical enhancements to ensure a website ranks higher and attracts more visitors, ultimately leading to increased exposure and potential business opportunities."
